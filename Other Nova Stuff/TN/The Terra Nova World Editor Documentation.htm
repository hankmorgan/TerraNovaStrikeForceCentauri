This is the beginning of the documentation of TN-Edit V0.70, EdMis V0.3 and their tools for modifing the textures of TN.


Features of the TN World Editor package:

  • Edit the terrain with a large number of powerful tools.
  • Place enemies, friends, buildings, trees, rocks, spacecrafts, trucks, hovercrafts and hidden objects (like Stone Heads).
  • Assign new weapons to your enemies. For example: Pirates can have Hegemony weaponry or fire at you with SFC weaponry.
  • Set the drop point and the pickup point to any location you like. For example: drop directly inside an hostile camp and start to struggle for survival!
  • Calculate the sunlight for any terrain you like: Play a game at sunrise or during the day, when sun is high.
  • Set the weather conditions to anything you like: Sunlight, Rain, Snow, Storm, Day, Night, ...
  • Set the gravity and the temperature of the planet.
  • TNEdit allows previewing the terrain you're editing in quite a number of ways. The most important one is the "In-Game-Graphics-Preview" which displays the terrain like it will be shown in the real game. Only restriction in version 0.70 is: Buildings and enemies won't be displayed. Take a look at these: Lake and forrest of TNDemo2 and Sunrise on New Hope.
  • Edit the text of the mission (de)briefings and the names of the objects in the game.
  • Extract and exchange any texture and graphic of Terra Nova.
  • All in all: You can design your own world!
